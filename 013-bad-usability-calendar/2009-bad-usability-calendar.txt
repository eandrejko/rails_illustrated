# 2009 Bad Usability Calendar Available

The [Bad Usability Calender](http://www.badusability.com/) for 2009 can now be downloaded.  Some of the lessons of 2009 include:

- "Content is king - fix it first!"
- "Make some choices for your users.  They don't need to decide on everything."
- "Just because it matters to you doesn’t mean it’s important to everyone else. Make some choices for your users."
- many more...
